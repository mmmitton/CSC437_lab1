Bulbasaur

Pokemon #: 0001

Type:

Grass / Poison

Description:

Bulbasaur (Japanese: フシギダネ Fushigidane) is a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves into Ivysaur starting at level 16, which evolves into Venusaur starting at level 32. Along with Charmander and Squirtle, Bulbasaur is one of the three starter Pokémon of Kanto availiable at the beginning of Pokémon Red, Green, Blue, FireRed, and LeafGreen.

Abilities:

  • Overgrow
  • Chlorophyll (Hidden Ability)
