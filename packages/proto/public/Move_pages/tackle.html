Tackle Normal Physical 40 100% 35

Description:

Tackle is a basic and commonly used Normal-type move in the Pokémon series. It is a physical move that involves the Pokémon physically charging and striking the opponent with force. Tackle has a base power of 40 and an accuracy of 100%. It is one of the earliest moves that Pokémon usually learn and is often considered a starting move for many Pokémon. Tackle is straightforward and doesn't have any additional effects like status conditions or stat changes. It is commonly used in the early stages of the game when Pokémon have limited move options. As a basic move, Tackle is not known for its high damage output, but it serves its purpose in the early battles of a Pokémon's journey.
